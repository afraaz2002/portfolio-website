Project features

Fully functional School Management app

Viewers can be aware of school's infrastructure

The school admin has the authority to view and remove an employee or a student

The school admin can also register the employees as well as the students

The employee(teacher) can view their profile which the admin created

The teacher role is to view/update student marks

Finally, the student can login with their account and can view their marks
