Steps and Challenges involved in making this project

  • Planning

    - Thinking of a good story
    - Planning the location and number of people required for the shoot

  • Setting up the project

    - Writing a script for the video
    - Assigning roles to the people
    - Discussing about screenplay and cinematography
    - Setting up the scene ready for shoot

  • Execution

    - Shooting scene by scene according to the script
    - Sorting and flitering the clips for the final video
    - Editing each clips with right fliters
    - Merging the finalized clips into a final video

  • Challenges Faced

    - Setting up the location
    - Shooting clips without disturbance
